FAQ

Will my files be safe? Does Neatify modify or delete anything?

Absolutely safe. Neatify only moves files, never edits, modifies, or deletes anything. Your files remain exactly the same, just organized. If you ever stop using Neatify, all your organized files stay right where they are.

Will Neatify slow down my Mac or use a lot of battery?

Neatify uses just 40-60MB of memory, lighter than a single browser tab. It runs silently in the background without affecting your Mac's speed, battery, or performance. You'll forget it's even there.

How long does it take to set up and see results?

Instant results. Download, grant folder access, and you're organizing files in under 2 minutes. The One-Click Organizer transforms messy folders immediately. Set up custom rules once, then just press Start whenever you want to start organizing.

Why does file organization cost money when I can do it manually?

Your manual sorting creates an exhausting cycle: organize for hours, watch chaos return within days, repeat endlessly. Each messy folder triggers mental fatigue and decision paralysis. Neatify breaks this cycle permanently, one click replaces hours of mind-numbing work.

What are the system requirements?

macOS 14+ (Sonoma). Works on both Apple Silicon and Intel Macs.
